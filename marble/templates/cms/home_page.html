Two Rock Software is a natural evolution

Two Rock Software is a natural evolution of the collaboration that began in 2012. Our founders began working together on a custom case management app for a small law firm in Chapel Hill, North Carolina. Both were already using Django and loved doing Python development.

After years of working together in various capacities on several different projects, they decided to found a new company together. The name comes from the fact that each of our founders have a name that means rock: Peter and Rocha.
